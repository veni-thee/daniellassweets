How to Order

Step 1. Decide on your order or obtain a photo of your desired cake design. (You can check designs online)

Step 2. Send us a meesage via our contact form , Facebook Messenger
or Instagram with your order details.

Step 3. Pick up your order or we can have it delivered to you.

Please note:

Prices vary and are subject to change depending on the size and the design.
Delivery is via Lalamove or Mr. Speedy.
It is advisable to book a car instead of motorcycle to avoid damage to the cakes.
Delivery charge is paid by the buyer.
